About NextGen Sites

Hi, I'm Henry — I run NextGen Sites and build websites for anyone who needs to get online without the hassle. Actors, artists, shop owners, freelancers, students, creators — if you need a site, I can help. My focus is simple layouts, fast loading pages, and designs that look great on phones.

Every site starts from a template you can preview below. I customize it with your info, brand colors, photos, and text so it feels like yours — not a generic cookie-cutter site.
